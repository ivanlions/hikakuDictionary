Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 3/17/2025

Thank you for using Hikaku Dictionary. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains what data is collected and how it is used.

1. Data Collection

The Hikaku Dictionary app itself does not collect personal data. However, we use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat may collect anonymous user data and purchase analytics, including:

  • Transaction history (e.g., purchases, refunds)
  • Anonymized user identifiers
  • Subscription status

For more details on how RevenueCat handles data, please refer to their Privacy Policy.

2. Data Usage

The data collected via RevenueCat is used solely for managing in-app purchases and improving the user experience. We do not sell, share, or use this data for advertising.

3. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@hikakuapp.xyz.
